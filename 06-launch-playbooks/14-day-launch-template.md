# 14-Day Launch Template

Day-by-day sequence from "ready to launch" to "live and selling." Use this as the master checklist for every launch.

---

## Days -14 to -8 (Week 1): Tease

**Goal:** Plant the problem in the audience's mind. Don't mention the product yet.

- [ ] Day -14: Post on primary platform — surface the problem the product solves
- [ ] Day -12: Newsletter — go deeper on the same problem, share a related insight
- [ ] Day -10: Post — share a counterintuitive POV related to the topic
- [ ] Day -8: Post — ask the audience a question that reveals the depth of the pain

**Output:** 3–4 pieces of content all orbiting the same problem. No mentions of "I'm building..." yet.

---

## Days -7 to -4: Pre-Announce + Waitlist

**Goal:** Capture intent. Build the launch list.

- [ ] Day -7: Post on primary platform — "I'm building something to solve [problem]. Reply if you want early access."
- [ ] Day -7: Newsletter — same message to the email list with a waitlist form link
- [ ] Day -7: Configure waitlist form (Gumroad subscribe form OR landing page with form)
- [ ] Day -6: Drop into 3–5 niche communities (Reddit, Slack, Discord) where appropriate, sharing the same waitlist
- [ ] Day -5: Post — share what's inside (rough cut)
- [ ] Day -4: Personal DMs — reach out to 10–20 people who'd be ideal early buyers

**Output:** Waitlist with at least 50 signups. Goal: 100+.

---

## Days -3 to -1: Final Prep

**Goal:** Everything is ready. No fires on launch day.

- [ ] Day -3: Run pre-launch checklist (see `pre-launch-checklist.md`)
- [ ] Day -3: Email waitlist — "Launching in 3 days. Founding price = $[X], goes up to $[Y] for everyone after."
- [ ] Day -2: Email waitlist — origin story / why I built this
- [ ] Day -1: Email waitlist — "Tomorrow, 9 AM EST"
- [ ] Day -1: Schedule launch day social posts
- [ ] Day -1: Test the full purchase flow yourself with a 100% discount code
- [ ] Day -1: Confirm post-purchase workflow fires correctly
- [ ] Day -1: Sleep. Don't launch tired.

---

## Day 0: Launch

**Goal:** Make a clear, confident announcement. Don't apologize for selling.

- [ ] 7:00 AM: Final test of the full flow
- [ ] 9:00 AM: Email goes out to entire list (waitlist gets it first by 30 min if segmentable)
- [ ] 9:15 AM: Post on primary platform — "It's live"
- [ ] 9:15 AM: Post on secondary platforms
- [ ] 10:00 AM: Reply to early questions in comments / DMs
- [ ] 12:00 PM: Mid-day post — share the first sale screenshot or a beta-tester quote
- [ ] 4:00 PM: Second email — "What's actually inside" (see `launch-sequence.md`)
- [ ] 8:00 PM: Last social post of the day — first-day numbers + thank-you to early buyers

**Founding price:** Active for 48 hours from this moment.

---

## Day 1: Momentum

- [ ] 9:00 AM: Email — testimonial / use case (see launch-sequence.md Email 3)
- [ ] Throughout: Reply to questions personally, share screenshots of buyer reactions, post wins
- [ ] Mid-day: Share to any community where appropriate (don't spam — share value, mention the product as part of the context)

## Day 2: Objection Handling

- [ ] 9:00 AM: Email — "But what if..." objection-handling email
- [ ] Mid-day: Post — common questions answered

## Day 3–4: Reinforce

- [ ] Day 3: Email — case study / specific outcome
- [ ] Day 4: Email — cost of inaction

## Day 5: Last 24 Hours

- [ ] 9:00 AM: Email — "Last call — founding price ends tomorrow night"
- [ ] 6:00 PM: Reminder post on social

## Day 6: Last Call

- [ ] 9:00 AM: Email — "Last call — tonight at midnight"
- [ ] 8:00 PM: Email — "4 hours left"
- [ ] 11:00 PM: Optional final post on social — short, just "tonight"
- [ ] 11:59 PM: Founding price expires. Update all references to full price.

## Day 7+: Post-Launch

- [ ] Day 7: Update sales page to full price
- [ ] Day 7: Disable founding-price discount code
- [ ] Day 7: Email new buyers a thank-you, ask first impressions
- [ ] Day 14: Post on social — total launch numbers (transparency builds trust for next launch)
- [ ] Day 30: Run post-launch iteration review (see `post-launch-iteration.md`)

---

## Revenue Distribution (Typical)

| Day | % of Total Launch Revenue |
|---|---|
| Day 0 | 25–35% |
| Day 1–4 | 25–35% |
| Day 5 (last day before deadline) | 10–15% |
| **Day 6 (last 24 hours)** | **30–40%** |

The last-call window is non-skippable. The reason most "launches" underperform is that creators get tired by Day 4 and stop sending.

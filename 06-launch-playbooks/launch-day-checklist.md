# Launch Day Checklist

Hour-by-hour for Day 0. Print this. Tape it to your monitor.

---

## 7:00 AM — Final Test

- [ ] Sales page loads
- [ ] Buy button works
- [ ] Discount code applies correctly at checkout
- [ ] Test purchase fires post-purchase Email 1
- [ ] Refund the test purchase (if real card used)

## 8:30 AM — Pre-Send Verification

- [ ] First launch email queued correctly in platform
- [ ] Subject line and preview text final
- [ ] Send test email to yourself, read it on phone
- [ ] Reply-to address is monitored

## 9:00 AM — Launch Email Sends

- [ ] Confirm the email is going out (not stuck in queue)
- [ ] Within 5 minutes, the first sale should land if the list is warm
- [ ] If no sale within 30 minutes: check the sales page is loading correctly

## 9:15 AM — Social Posts

- [ ] Primary platform post live
- [ ] Secondary platform posts live
- [ ] LinkedIn / X / wherever the audience is

## 10:00–11:00 AM — First Wave

- [ ] Reply to comments and DMs personally
- [ ] Quote-tweet / repost any organic mentions
- [ ] Watch sales feed — first wave usually peaks here

## 12:00 PM — Mid-Day Push

- [ ] Post first-day numbers OR a beta-tester quote
- [ ] DM 5 ideal-fit people who haven't bought yet — share the link, no pressure

## 4:00 PM — Second Email

- [ ] Email 2 of launch sequence sends ("What's actually inside")
- [ ] Reply to any new questions

## 6:00 PM — Engagement Check

- [ ] Check social comments, reply to all questions
- [ ] Refresh sales page metrics

## 8:00 PM — End of Day Update

- [ ] Post a thank-you to early buyers
- [ ] Share top question + answer from the day
- [ ] Confirm tomorrow's email is queued

## End of Day — Close-Out

- [ ] Note total day-1 sales
- [ ] Note any technical issues / friction points
- [ ] Note any objections that came up repeatedly (these go into Day 2's email)
- [ ] Sleep.

---

## What to Do If...

**No sales in the first hour:**
- Check the sales page is loading
- Check the email actually sent
- Check the buy button works
- If all is fine, wait. Some lists are slow openers.

**A bug surfaces (broken link, wrong price, etc.):**
- Fix immediately
- If it affected anyone, email those buyers personally with the fix
- Don't apologize publicly unless the bug actually hurt someone

**A high-profile person buys:**
- Reply personally with a thank-you
- Don't ask for a testimonial on Day 0 — too soon
- Note for Day 30 testimonial outreach

**Someone publicly criticizes the launch:**
- Reply once, calmly, on the merits
- Don't engage further if they escalate
- Most criticism is opportunity for a clarification post that drives sales

---

## Energy Management

Launch days are emotionally intense. The amplitude of "selling something I made" is high.

- Don't refresh sales metrics every 5 minutes — set a cadence (every 2 hours)
- Eat. Drink water. Stand up. The dopamine swings will exhaust you.
- Don't make pricing or product changes mid-day. If something is wrong, finish the day, then fix.

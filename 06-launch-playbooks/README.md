# 06 · Launch Playbooks

A launch is a compressed, manufactured event of attention. Even for evergreen products, doing 4–6 launches per year drives more revenue than passive evergreen selling alone.

## Files in this folder

- [`14-day-launch-template.md`](./14-day-launch-template.md) — The full 14-day sequence from "ready to launch" to "live."
- [`pre-launch-checklist.md`](./pre-launch-checklist.md) — Everything that must be true before launch day.
- [`launch-day-checklist.md`](./launch-day-checklist.md) — Hour-by-hour for launch day itself.
- [`post-launch-iteration.md`](./post-launch-iteration.md) — The 7-day post-launch review and the 90-day deep review.
- `by-product/<slug>.md` — Per-product launch playbooks, customized.

## Launch Cadence

- **First launch:** 14-day sequence, founding price, full effort
- **Mid-launches:** 60–90 days later, often tied to a new bonus, repositioning, or seasonal hook
- **Evergreen:** Product stays live at full price between launches; new subscribers see it via the welcome sequence

## When NOT to Launch

- Major holiday weekends (Thanksgiving, Christmas, July 4) — open rates tank
- Friday afternoon → Sunday — empirically the worst window for B2B digital products
- Mid-summer (last 2 weeks of August in the US) — vacation slump

Best windows: **Tuesday–Thursday, 9–11 AM EST**, January–April or September–November.

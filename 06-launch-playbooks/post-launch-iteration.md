Run on Day 30. Goal: assess product-market fit and plan iteration.

| Buyers who completed a testimonial | | |

### Strategic Questions

1. **Did this product hit a starving crowd?** (revenue per traffic source, conversion rate, buyer enthusiasm)

2. **What did buyers ask for that we didn't include?** (these are the seeds of bonus updates and product #2)

3. **What language did buyers use to describe the value?** (mine for sales page rewrites and next-product positioning)

4. **What's the most common "I wish this also covered..." response?**

5. **If we were re-launching this product fresh tomorrow, what would we change?**

### Outputs

- [ ] Decision: re-launch with fixes (60–90 days) or move on
- [ ] List of additions / fixes for v2 of this product
- [ ] Initial concept for product #2 (informed by buyer feedback)
- [ ] Updated value ladder (does this slot match what we expected?)

## 90-Day Deep Review

Run on Day 90. Goal: strategic input for catalog expansion.

### The Forte Question

Tiago Forte's framing: **"What sold? Why? What didn't? What did buyers ask for that I didn't include?"** That last answer is product #2.

### Bundle Decision

If at least 2 other products have shipped by Day 90, evaluate bundling:
- [ ] Bundle value math: 3 PDFs × $49 = $147 unbundled, $97 bundled
- [ ] Common buyer overlap: do buyers of #1 typically also need #2 and #3?
- [ ] Repositioning the bundle as a "system" or "stack" instead of a discount

### Repositioning

- [ ] Has the avatar shifted based on actual buyers?
- [ ] Is the headline still the strongest framing, or has the language evolved?
- [ ] Any sales page section that needs a rewrite based on 90 days of data?

### Pricing Decision

- [ ] Time to raise prices? (Welsh's pattern: every ~250 sales, price goes up 15–30%)
- [ ] Add a second tier (premium bonus pack, walkthrough video version)?
- [ ] Decoy / anchor product on the sales page (referencing the next-rung product visibly)?

### Product #2 Brief

By the end of the 90-day review, you should have a 1-page brief for product #2 ready to drop into `/01-market-research/by-product/<new-slug>/`.

# Waitlist Sequence

Sent during the 7-day pre-launch window to people who joined the waitlist. Job: build anticipation, qualify the buyer, lock in priority access.

---

## Email 1 — Day -7: Confirm + Promise

**Subject:** You're on the list — here's what's coming

> You're in. Quick context on what I'm building and why:
>
> [2 paragraphs: the problem, your moment of "this needs to exist", the rough shape of the solution.]
>
> You'll be the first to know when it goes live, and you'll get a 48-hour founding price the public won't see.
>
> While you wait, [tease one piece of the framework — a specific insight or counterintuitive idea]. Curious to hear if this resonates — hit reply.

## Email 2 — Day -4: Behind the Build

**Subject:** What's inside (rough cut)

> Quick update on [Product Name]:
>
> Here's what's coming in:
> - [Component 1]
> - [Component 2]
> - [Component 3]
>
> Plus three bonuses I haven't announced yet.
>
> Launch is [date]. Founding price for waitlist subscribers only: $[X]. Goes up to $[Y] for everyone after.

## Email 3 — Day -2: Story / Origin

**Subject:** The mistake that made me build this

> [3-paragraph story: the specific failure or realization that made you understand the problem at a deep level. Vulnerability + specifics = trust.]
>
> [Product Name] is what I wish I'd had then.
>
> [Date], 9 AM EST. Watch your inbox.

## Email 4 — Day -1: Final Heads-Up

**Subject:** Tomorrow, 9 AM

> Heads up — [Product Name] goes live tomorrow at 9 AM EST.
>
> Founding price: $[X] for 48 hours.
>
> Tomorrow morning the link will be in your inbox. That's it.

---

## Configuration

In Gumroad: trigger = "New subscriber" filtered by signup tag/source = waitlist.
Or in ConvertKit/Beehiiv: tag-based segment that gets this sequence on join.

After launch sequence ends, the launch sequence (`launch-sequence.md`) takes over.

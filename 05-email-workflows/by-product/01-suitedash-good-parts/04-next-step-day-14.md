## Why This Works

- Frames the next-rung product as a response to a real, named pain (not a generic upsell)
- Two ladder rungs presented (Setup Service @ $497 + Operator Cohort @ $997/qtr) so buyer self-selects
- Reply-to-engage instead of click-to-buy = lower-pressure, higher-trust
- Reinforces guarantee at Day 14 (still 16 days of refund window remaining) = trust signal
- Anti-pushy framing ("if the work has done its job, the work has done its job") = aligns with brand voice

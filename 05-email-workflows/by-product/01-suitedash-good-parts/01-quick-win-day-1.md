## Body

> Hey [first name],
>
> If you only have 15 minutes with The Good Parts of SuiteDash today, here's what to do:
>
> Open Chapter 4 — The Configuration Order. Read just the 7-step list. Don't read the surrounding prose yet.
>
> Then open SuiteDash. Run Step 1 only: branding + subdomain. Logo (light + dark variants), primary color, custom subdomain. 10 minutes max.
>
> That's it for today. One step. Real progress. The thing most people get wrong is trying to do all 7 steps in one sitting on Day 1, fumbling on Step 3, and abandoning the whole protocol. Don't do that.
>
> Tomorrow: Step 2 (CRM custom fields). Day after: Step 3. By Day 7 you'll be live.
>
> Or — if you have a 90-minute uninterrupted block — open Bonus 1 (the Lock-In Protocol) and run all seven steps in one sitting. Either path works. Pick the one that fits your week.
>
> — Ike

---

## Why This Works

- Lowers the activation threshold to 15 minutes (one step, not seven)
- Directly addresses the abandonment failure mode (trying to do all 7 in one sitting and bailing on Step 3)
- Offers two paths (incremental vs. one-block) so the buyer self-selects based on calendar reality
- No CTA back to the sales page, no upsell — pure implementation focus

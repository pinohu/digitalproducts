- Concrete case study at Day 7 — when implementation momentum is most likely to stall
- Specific outcomes (3 templates, 3 clients onboarded) instead of vague claims
- Tees up the testimonial ask without pushing it (Day 30 email handles the formal ask)
- Acknowledges the buyer might still be mid-deployment (anti-shame framing)

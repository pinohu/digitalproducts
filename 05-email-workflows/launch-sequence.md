# Launch Email Sequence

The 7-day launch sequence to send to the email list when a new product goes live. Drives 60–80% of total launch revenue.

**Total emails:** 6 over 7 days.
**Last-day "last call" email** typically does 30–40% of total launch revenue all by itself. Don't skip it.

---

## Pre-Launch (Day -7 to -1)

Not in this sequence — covered in `waitlist-sequence.md`. Tease the problem, build curiosity, capture waitlist signups.

---

## Email 1 — Launch Day, AM: The Reveal

**Subject:** It's live: [Product Name]

**First line preview:** The thing I've been hinting at for the last week...

**Body:**

> Hey [first name],
>
> The thing I've been hinting at is live: **[Product Name]**.
>
> [One paragraph: what it is, who it's for, the specific outcome it delivers.]
>
> [One paragraph: why I built this — the moment it became obvious it needed to exist.]
>
> Founding price: **$[X]** for the next 48 hours. After that it goes to $[Y].
>
> Get it here: [link]
>
> Reply with any questions — I'll respond personally.
>
> [Name]

## Email 4 — Day 4: Objection Handling

**Subject:** "But what if..."

**First line preview:** Three questions came up a lot today...

**Body:**

> Hey [first name],
>
> Three questions kept coming up today:
>
> **"What if I'm a beginner?"** → [Honest answer]
>
> **"What if I already use [tool]?"** → [Honest answer]
>
> **"What if it doesn't work for me?"** → 30-day money-back guarantee. Email me, full refund, no hoops. The product converts well enough that I'd rather have you try it and refund than hesitate.
>
> Get [Product Name]: [link]
>
> [Name]

## Email 7 — Last Day, Evening: 4 Hours Left

**Subject:** 4 hours

**First line preview:** Tonight at midnight...

**Body:**

> Hey [first name],
>
> 4 hours until the founding price on [Product Name] expires.
>
> $[X] → $[Y] at midnight.
>
> [link]
>
> [Name]

**Why this works:** This is intentionally short. People who haven't bought by now respond to urgency, not more pitch. Brevity = urgency.

---

## Last-Call Email Math

In a typical launch:
- Day 1: 30–40% of sales
- Day 2–4: 20–30% of sales
- Day 5: 10–15% of sales
- **Day 6 last-call (final 24 hours): 30–40% of sales**

The last-call email is *the single highest-revenue email of the entire sequence*. Don't soft-pedal it. Don't apologize for sending it. Don't worry about being annoying.

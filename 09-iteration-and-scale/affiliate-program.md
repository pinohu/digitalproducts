# Affiliate Program

Other creators sell your product to their audience for a commission. Done right, affiliates can drive 30–50% of sales without paid ads.

## When to Launch

After a product has at least 50 sales of its own. Affiliates won't promote a product that hasn't proven it converts.

## Commission Structure

**Default: 30%** of every sale, attributed to the affiliate's referral link.

For high-effort affiliates (newsletter mentions, dedicated content):
- 40–50% on first 30 days of the launch window

For low-effort affiliates (passive link drops):
- 30% standing offer

Don't go below 25%. It signals the product doesn't have margin to share.

Don't go above 50% on standing offers. You'll lose money on customer acquisition once you account for refunds, support, and platform fees.

## Setup in Gumroad

1. Toggle on the affiliate program for the product
2. Set the commission percentage
3. Affiliates apply via your storefront link
4. You approve manually (recommended) or auto-approve
5. Affiliates get a unique link to share

## Recruiting Affiliates

The first 5–10 affiliates are recruited by hand. After that, the program markets itself.

**First-wave outreach:**
- People in your niche with audiences of 1K–50K
- People who've already mentioned you organically
- People who run complementary products to yours
- Past buyers who loved the product and have an audience

**Outreach template:**
> Hey [name],
>
> Big fan of [their work / specific thing they did]. Quick note: I just launched [Product Name] — [one-line pitch] — and I think it'd be a good fit for your audience.
>
> I'm running an affiliate program: 30% commission per sale, with a 50% bump for the first 30 days if you do a newsletter mention or dedicated post.
>
> If you're interested, here's the link to grab your affiliate code: [link]
>
> Either way — keep up the great work.
>
> [Name]

## Affiliate Resources

Once an affiliate is approved, give them:
- Their unique link
- 3–5 swipe copy templates (LinkedIn post, X thread, email mention, newsletter blurb)
- A bank of high-quality images / quotes from the product
- A FAQ for common questions their audience might ask
- Your direct contact for support

This makes promotion frictionless. The easier you make it, the more affiliates actually post.

## Tracking and Payment

- Gumroad tracks attribution automatically
- Payouts: monthly, after a 30-day refund window
- Send affiliates a brief monthly email with their stats and earnings (transparency = retention)

## What Drives Affiliate Performance

The single biggest driver: **how proud they are to recommend the product.** Affiliates who genuinely use the product themselves convert at 5–10x the rate of affiliates who don't.

Implication: send free copies of every product to your top 20 potential affiliates. Cost: ~$0. Return: massive.

## Anti-Patterns

- ❌ Forcing affiliates to use a specific copy template (kills authenticity)
- ❌ Making affiliates wait 60–90 days for payouts (kills retention)
- ❌ Cutting affiliate commissions on existing affiliates (broken trust forever)
- ❌ Aggressive outreach to people who haven't engaged with your work (looks spammy)

## What not to do

- Do not write more generic SuiteDash commentary just to make the PDF longer.
- Do not claim launch proof that does not exist yet.
- Do not create a new primary product family sprint until the current ladder is either shipped or explicitly deprioritized.

## Review cadence

- 7-day review: confirm whether proof and Gumroad blockers narrowed.
- 30-day review: confirm whether the tripwire produced proof, toolkit interest, or implementation demand.
- 90-day review: decide whether to keep this as an evergreen front-door product, bundle it, or fold it into a broader client-portal operating system.

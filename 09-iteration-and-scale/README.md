## The Iteration Hierarchy

When deciding what to do next:

1. **First, optimize the current product.** Sales page rewrite > new product. A 2x conversion lift on an existing product beats a brand-new product 10 times out of 10.
2. **Then, build the bundle.** Three products × $49 each = $147 unbundled / $97 bundled. The bundle lifts AOV without requiring a new product.
3. **Then, build the variation.** Same product, different niche framing. ("The Good Parts of SuiteDash" → "The Good Parts of SuiteDash for Coaches" → "...for Agencies" → "...for Service Pros".)
4. **Only then, build the next product from scratch.**

This hierarchy avoids the most common solopreneur mistake: building product after product without ever fully extracting the value of the first one.

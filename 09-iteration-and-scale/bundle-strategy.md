# Bundle Strategy

After 2–3 products are live, bundling becomes the single fastest revenue lift available. Implementation: <2 hours. Revenue lift: typically 30–50% of bundle-sized buyer cohort.

## When to Bundle

- After at least 2 products are live and have generated at least 50 sales each
- When the products solve adjacent problems for the same avatar
- When buyers of Product A regularly ask "what about [topic of Product B]?"

Don't bundle if:
- The products serve different avatars (the bundle confuses positioning)
- One product is significantly weaker than the others (the weaker product drags the bundle's perceived value)
- Total bundle price would be unbelievably low for the value (pushes into "must be a scam" territory)

## Bundle Pricing Math

The default rule: **Bundle price = ~66% of the sum of individual prices.**

| Setup | Math |
|---|---|
| 3 PDFs × $49 each | $147 unbundled |
| 3-PDF bundle | $97 (33% off) |
| AOV lift vs single | 2x (from $49 to $97) |
| Lost revenue per bundle vs full sum | $50 |

The bundle math is *not* "discount our products." The bundle math is **"buyers who would have bought one are now buying three at a higher AOV."** That's a 2x AOV gain.

## Bundle Naming

A bundle is not "3 PDFs at a discount." It's **a system**.

❌ "The Dynasty PDF Bundle (3 PDFs, 33% off)"
✅ "The Dynasty Operator's Stack — Everything You Need to Run a Tech-Forward Solo Business"

The bundle should feel like a curated package with its own identity. Sometimes it gets its own sales page.

## Bundle Sales Page

Either:
- **Update individual sales pages** to feature the bundle as the recommended option (single-page strategy)
- **Build a separate bundle sales page** (like `/bundles/operator-stack`)

The single-page strategy is simpler. The separate-page strategy converts better but takes more time.

## Order Bumps and Upsells

In Gumroad and ThriveCart, you can offer:
- **Order bump:** "Add Product B for +$30" right at checkout
- **Upsell:** "After buying A, add the bundle for +$48"

Order bumps typically convert 20–30% of buyers and add ~20% to total revenue per launch. Setup time: <1 hour. Use these aggressively.

## When to Build the Second Bundle

Once products 4–6 ship, build a second 3-pack bundle. Keep the first bundle live (don't replace it).

Once 6 products ship, consider the **mega-bundle**: all 6 products at $297 with a setup-call bonus. Position as "the complete stack" — different avatar (someone willing to buy in bulk).

## Anti-Patterns

- ❌ Bundling unrelated products to inflate price (destroys trust)
- ❌ Permanent bundle discounts that never expire (poisons the standalone prices)
- ❌ Free trial bundles (paid bundles convert better than free ones)
- ❌ "All-access" lifetime memberships (kills future product launch revenue)

## The Stack (Default for Dynasty Empire)

- **Primary platform:** LinkedIn (B2B operator audience lives there)
- **Secondary platform:** X / Twitter (technical builders, faster iteration on content)
- **Long-form anchor:** ikeohu.com (essays, the six-lane authority strategy)
- **Email list:** Newsletter via ikeohu.com / future Beehiiv migration
- **Lead magnet:** Free PDF tied to the most-discussed problem in the niche

## The Compounding Asset

The email list is the only audience asset we fully own. Social platforms can throttle reach overnight; algorithms shift; accounts get suspended. The email list is portable, durable, and converts at 5–10x the rate of cold traffic.

**Every piece of content's primary job is to drive list signups.** Product sales follow.

## What This Folder Is *Not*

It's not a content marketing course. It's the operating instructions for *our specific* traffic strategy, designed around our specific avatar and primary platform. If the avatar shifts, this entire folder gets revisited.

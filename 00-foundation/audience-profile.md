# Audience Profile

## Primary Avatar — "The Technical Operator"

**Who they are:**
- Solopreneur, freelancer, agency owner, or technical founder
- Already running multiple tools (CRM, hosting, email, automation)
- 5+ years into building. Not a beginner. Not a billionaire.
- Annual revenue: $50K–$500K (the band where compression matters most)
- Time-starved. Tool-rich. Looking for *configured systems*, not raw information.

**What they want:**
- To stop being the bottleneck in their own business
- To turn one-off knowledge into reusable assets
- To monetize infrastructure they already own
- To avoid the agency overhead (high cost) and the "no-code course" trap (too shallow)

**What keeps them up:**
- "I have 50 tools and none of them talk to each other."
- "I keep building bespoke automations from scratch every time."
- "I'm sitting on $30K of LTDs and not getting $3K in actual ROI."
- "I can technically do this, but I don't have time."

**Where they congregate:**
- AppSumo Plus / lifetime deals communities
- Indie Hackers
- LinkedIn (operator / SaaS / agency niches)
- r/SaaS, r/Entrepreneur, r/Solopreneur
- SubTo / creative finance / boring-business acquisition circles
- SuiteDash / Brilliant Directories / specific tool-power-user communities

## What They Will Pay For

| Format | Willing to Pay | Why |
|---|---|---|
| 30-page tactical PDF | $19–$49 | Specific configuration knowledge, transcribed |
| Notion / Airtable / SuiteDash template pack | $49–$197 | Plug-and-play system |
| Walkthrough video + template | $97–$297 | Compression of months of trial-and-error |
| Done-with-you setup call | $297–$997 | Time savings + accountability |
| Cohort or community | $997+/yr | Peer access + ongoing access |

## What They Will *Not* Pay For

- Generic "start a business" content
- Mindset / motivation / "high-performance" courses
- Anything that smells like an MLM or "passive income" hype
- Tools they have to *build themselves from scratch*

## Language Patterns (Use in Copy)

**They say** → **We say**
- "I want passive income" → ❌ Not our reader.
- "I'm tired of cobbling things together" → ✅ Our reader.
- "I need to systemize this" → ✅
- "I have the tools, I just need the playbook" → ✅✅✅
- "I want to automate this" → ✅
- "I want to scale" → ✅ (but be specific about what)

## Anti-Avatar (Refuse to Sell To)

- Pure beginners who haven't shipped anything yet
- "I want to make money online" no-skill seekers
- People looking for done-for-you with $0 budget
- Anyone whose first question is "but does it really work?"

These aren't bad people; they're just not who we serve. Selling to them creates refunds, support load, and bad reviews.

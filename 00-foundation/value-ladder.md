# Value Ladder

The full price ladder from free to $5K+. Every product slot is a rung. The job of every rung is to qualify buyers for the next rung.

## The Ladder

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│ Rung 0  │ Free lead magnet (newsletter signup)          │  → captures email
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ Rung 1  │ $49 single PDF (trust tripwire)               │  → buyer #1 ever
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ Rung 2  │ $97 PDF bundle (3 PDFs)                       │  → AOV lift
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ Rung 3  │ $297 PDF + walkthrough video bundle           │  → premium info product
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ Rung 4  │ $497 system pack (PDF + Notion + templates)   │  → done-for-you assets
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ Rung 5  │ $997 done-with-you setup (1:1, 60–90 min)     │  → time-for-money premium
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ Rung 6  │ $2,997+ Dynasty operator cohort               │  → group transformation
├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
│ Rung 7  │ $5K+ done-for-you implementation              │  → full delegation
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

## Rung Logic

**Rung 0 — Lead magnet.** Free, but valuable enough to generate organic referrals. Job: build the email list. Conversion target: 30%+ of qualified site visitors.

**Rung 1 — $49 trust tripwire.** Welsh's discovery: price low enough to be impulse, deliver 100x value, prime for everything that comes after. Refund rate target: <5%. Buyer-to-rung-2 conversion target: 15–25% within 60 days.

**Rung 2 — $97 bundle.** Activates after the buyer is already inside the workflow. Bundles consistently lift AOV 1.5–2x.

**Rung 3 — $297 video bundle.** Same content, richer format. Targets buyers who want compression of *time* not just information. Higher margin.

**Rung 4 — $497 system pack.** Adds plug-and-play assets (Notion templates, automations, swipe files). The buyer is no longer just learning; they're installing.

**Rung 5 — $997 done-with-you.** First time-for-money rung. Limited capacity creates urgency. Use as a filter for who graduates to Rung 6.

**Rung 6 — Cohort/community.** Recurring revenue. The lifestyle-business holy grail. Welsh's $199/yr community at scale, our version is positioned higher.

**Rung 7 — Done-for-you.** Custom builds. Pace these intentionally — they're high revenue but high time cost. Use them to fund Rung 4 product expansion.

## Movement Rules

- A buyer can skip rungs upward (someone may go straight from Rung 0 → Rung 5).
- A buyer should *never* be sold the rung above the one they just bought in the same email — wait at least 7 days.
- Every rung must reference the rung above it inside the product itself ("when you're ready for X, here's the next step").
- Refunds/cancellations from a higher rung do not bump someone down — they get a "we're here when you're ready" exit, not a downsell pitch.

## Lifetime Value Targets

| Buyer Cohort | Target LTV | Realistic by Year |
|---|---|---|
| Rung 1 only | $49 | Year 1 |
| Rung 1 + Rung 2 | $146 | Year 1 |
| Through Rung 4 | $940 | Year 1–2 |
| Through Rung 6 | $4,000+ | Year 2–3 |

## What This Ladder Is *Not*

It is not a forced funnel. Buyers move at their own pace. The ladder exists so that *when* they're ready to spend more, the next rung is sitting there ready — not so we're spamming them up the rungs against their will.

## The Final Offer Statement

Read like ad copy:

> *For [avatar], who want [dream outcome], we're offering [product name] — [one-sentence promise].*
>
> *You get [core], plus [bonus 1], plus [bonus 2], plus [bonus 3]. Total value: $[X]. Today: $[Y].*
>
> *And if [outcome doesn't happen] within [timeframe], [guarantee].*
>
> *Founding price expires [when].*

---

## Anti-Pattern Check

- [ ] **One outcome, one promise** — not "this does 50 things"
- [ ] **Specific** — not "boost your business"
- [ ] **Bonuses each address an objection** — not generic add-ons
- [ ] **Risk reversal is unconditional or fairly conditional** — not "no refunds"
- [ ] **Urgency is real** — not manufactured countdown timer fraud
- [ ] **Value ratio ≥ 10x** — not 2x or 3x

# Value Equation Worksheet

Hormozi's Value Equation, applied to one specific product.

```
              Dream Outcome × Perceived Likelihood
   Value  =  ──────────────────────────────────────
                  Time Delay × Effort & Sacrifice
```

The way to maximize value is to push the numerator up *and* push the denominator down. Most creators only think about the numerator. The denominator is where the leverage hides.

## Anti-Patterns

- **Inflating the dream outcome with hype.** Buyers are sophisticated. "Make $10K/month in your first week" reads as scam. Be specific and credible.
- **Faking certainty with vague guarantees.** "100% satisfaction guaranteed!!!" without specifics decreases trust. A specific, honest guarantee increases it.
- **Adding more content to "increase value."** More content usually *increases* time delay and effort, which lowers value. Less content with better outcomes wins.
- **Promising zero effort.** If it sounds too easy, the perceived likelihood drops. Honest effort framing ("90 minutes of focused work") beats "totally automatic" hype.

# Guarantee Templates

Risk reversal language ready to drop into any sales page. Pick one. Customize the specifics. Never ship without a guarantee.

## Why Guarantees Work

- Empirical refund rate on digital products with guarantees: **2–5%**
- Conversion lift from including a guarantee: **3–5x**
- The math is not close — the lift dwarfs the refunds, every time.

## Standard 30-Day Money-Back

> **30-Day Money-Back Guarantee.** If for any reason you don't find this useful, email me within 30 days of purchase and I'll refund every penny. No questions, no forms, no hoops. Just reply to your purchase receipt and write "refund." Done.

**Use for:** Default. Almost all products.

---

## Conditional / "Action Guarantee"

> **The Action Guarantee.** Apply the [specific framework / checklist / template] from this product within 14 days. If you've actually applied it and aren't seeing [specific outcome], email me your work, and I'll either personally help you fix it OR refund you in full. Your call.

**Use for:** Higher-priced products where you want to filter out non-implementers.

---

## Double-Your-Money-Back

> **The Double-Down Guarantee.** I'm so confident this works that if you complete the entire [process / checklist / framework] in 30 days and don't see [specific quantifiable outcome], I'll refund your purchase AND send you an additional $[X] for your time. You'd have to actively work to lose money on this.

**Use for:** Premium offers where you want to demonstrate maximum confidence. Use sparingly.

---

## "Better Than Free" Guarantee

> **The Better-Than-Free Guarantee.** Try the entire product for 30 days. If you decide it's not for you, ask for a refund — and keep [specific bonus]. That bonus alone is worth $[X], so the worst-case scenario is you make money on this transaction.

**Use for:** Products with a particularly strong standalone bonus you're willing to give away.

---

## "Specific Outcome" Guarantee

> **The [Outcome] Guarantee.** If you don't [specific, measurable outcome] within [specific timeframe] of completing this product, I'll refund your purchase in full.

**Use for:** Products where the outcome is highly measurable. Risky to use unless you're genuinely confident.

---

## "We'll Make It Work" Guarantee

> **The "We'll Make It Work" Guarantee.** If you hit a wall and can't get this to work for your situation, email me. I'll personally help you troubleshoot — included free with your purchase. If we still can't make it work, you get a full refund. Either way, you don't stay stuck.

**Use for:** Higher-touch products where you're willing to provide some support.

---

## What NOT to Do

- ❌ "All sales final, no refunds" — destroys conversion. The refund rate from no-guarantee products is often *higher* (charge-backs).
- ❌ "Refunds at our discretion" — reads as "we'll fight you." Just say yes or no.
- ❌ Burying the guarantee in fine print — put it on the sales page in 24pt type.
- ❌ Adding hoops ("must complete a 10-question form, must wait 14 business days") — reads as you not actually wanting to honor it. Make refunds painless.

## The Refund Email Response (Save This)

When someone asks for a refund, respond within 24 hours with:

> Hey [name],
>
> No problem — refund is processed. You should see it back on your card within 3–5 business days.
>
> If you have a moment: what made it not the right fit? I'm not asking to talk you out of it — I genuinely want to know so I can make it better for the next person.
>
> Either way, thanks for giving it a shot. Keep [bonus / freebie they downloaded] if you want — no need to delete.
>
> [Name]

This response:
- Refunds first, asks questions second
- Actually asks for feedback
- Leaves the bonus in their hands (turns a refund into goodwill)
- Doesn't apologize / grovel / try to retain

The data from this email is gold. It's the highest-signal feedback you'll ever get.

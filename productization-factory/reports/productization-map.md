# Productization Map

This report is the human-readable front door for the Productization Factory.

## First-priority recommendation

- First product family: `Client Portal & Service Delivery OS`
- First anchor product: `The Good Parts of SuiteDash`

Why:

- the repo already has active sprint momentum here
- the AppSumo stack is heavily concentrated in client portal and service-delivery tools
- this family ladders naturally into setup sprints and recurring operations retainers

## Operating rule

If a proposed product sounds like a tutorial for one owned tool, it is probably not a strong candidate yet.

Reframe it as:

- a buyer
- a painful workflow
- a promised outcome
- a trust tripwire
- an implementation or recurring service ladder

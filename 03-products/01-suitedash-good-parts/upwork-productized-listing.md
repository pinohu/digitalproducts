## Why This Variant Exists

- The PDF at $49 captures DIY buyers.
- Sensei partners at $125/hr or $400+ project rates capture done-for-you buyers.
- **A budget gap exists between those two tiers.** Buyers searching "SuiteDash help" on Upwork with $50–150 in mind currently have no good option — Senseis are too expensive, freelancers without expertise are a roll of the dice.

This $97 listing captures that segment. The PDF does the heavy lifting; the call resolves whatever the PDF didn't, and lets the buyer leave a public review on Upwork that compounds over time.

## What Happens After Purchase (Operationally)

1. **Order received** — Upwork notifies via email + dashboard
2. **Same-day delivery (within 4 hours)** — Send via Upwork message:
   - All 4 PDFs (core + 3 bonuses) attached
   - Calendly link to book the 30-min call
   - One-line note: *"PDF is yours immediately; book the call when you've had a chance to read it. Most buyers schedule for 3–7 days out."*
3. **Pre-call** — Receive their Calendly intake form (asks: license tier, hours spent so far, biggest current blocker)
4. **Call delivery** — 30 minutes on Zoom. Camera on. Walk through their specific situation. End with a personalized configuration plan written into the chat as bullet points.
5. **Post-call** — Send a 1-page summary of recommendations within 24 hours. Request Upwork review.

Total operator time per order: ~45 minutes (call + summary). Effective hourly: ~$130/hr — slightly above Sensei rate, with no acquisition cost since Upwork search delivers the buyer.

## Inventory / Capacity Limits

Don't list this without thinking about cap.

- **5 orders per week is sustainable** alongside other Dynasty work
- **10 orders per week starts to compete with PA CROP / launches**
- **Beyond 10/week:** raise the price to $147 or pause the listing

If demand exceeds capacity, that's signal to develop:
- A group call format (4–6 people, $147/seat, same prep, 60 minutes)
- A async-only "PDF + written 1-page plan" variant ($67, no call)

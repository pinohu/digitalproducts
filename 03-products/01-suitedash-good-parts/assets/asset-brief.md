# Asset Brief — The Good Parts of SuiteDash

Status: v1 cover/hero/social assets generated from this brief and exported to `deliverables/`; editable SVG source stored in `assets/`. Assets are abstract and avoid SuiteDash logos/screenshots.

## Product positioning

An operator-grade, no-fluff guide for SuiteDash owners who want the useful path through the platform without studying every feature.

## Visual direction

- Clean operator dashboard, not creator-course fluff.
- Technical but approachable.
- Emphasize compression: many modules → one clear path.
- Avoid screenshots that imply official SuiteDash affiliation unless rights/permissions are confirmed.

## Core visual metaphor

A cluttered all-in-one platform map being reduced into a highlighted 7-module route.

## Cover text

Title:
The Good Parts of SuiteDash

Subtitle:
The 90-minute operator path through the 20% of SuiteDash that creates 80% of the client-ops value.

Badge ideas:
- Operator Cut
- 7 Modules • 12 Skips • 2 Automations
- PDF + 3 Bonuses

## Gumroad hero requirements

- Size: 1280 x 720 px.
- Must be readable as a small card.
- Avoid dense text.
- Include title, subtitle or badge, and simple route/map motif.

## Social card requirements

- Size: 1200 x 630 px.
- Copy variant: “Stop wandering through SuiteDash. Configure the good parts first.”
- CTA: “Founding price: $29 / Public: $49” only if pricing is reconciled before launch.

## Suggested palette

- Background: deep navy / charcoal.
- Accent: electric blue or green for the “good parts” path.
- Secondary: off-white text.
- Warning/skip accent: muted amber or red for kill-list references.

## Design notes

- Include abstract module tiles labeled CRM, Portal, Intake, Projects, Files, Billing, Automations.
- Show non-core modules faded or crossed out in the background.
- Keep SuiteDash name as product subject, not as a logo lockup.
- Add a small “by Dynasty Empire” or creator mark only if brand assets are finalized.

# Product 01 — The Good Parts of SuiteDash

> *Status:* **In progress** — Day 1 of 14-day sprint
> *Target ship:* T+14 from sprint start
> *Source:* SuiteDash repo (existing accumulated knowledge)
> *Price:* $49 (trust tripwire)
> *Platform:* Gumroad

## One-Sentence Pitch

> *The 20% of SuiteDash features that drive 80% of the value, surfaced for technical operators who want to deploy fast without sitting through 40 hours of platform documentation.*

## The Avatar

A SuiteDash subscriber who:
- Bought one or more Pinnacle licenses (often via AppSumo LTD)
- Has spent 5–40 hours poking at the platform without a clear deployment
- Is technically capable but time-starved
- Wants a *configured opinionated path*, not a feature reference

## The Promise

> Read this, and within 90 minutes you'll know:
> - The 7 modules in SuiteDash that actually matter (and which 12 to skip)
> - The configuration order that prevents 80% of common rebuild scenarios
> - The two automation patterns that pay back the price of the platform in week one
> - When to use SuiteDash vs. when to use a separate purpose-built tool

## Why This Is Product #1

- Source material already exists in the SuiteDash repo (no research lift)
- Audience already validated (Ike's network is full of SuiteDash LTD holders)
- Price point ($49) hits the trust-tripwire sweet spot
- Naturally ladders to higher-rung products (SuiteDash setup service, Dynasty operator cohort)
